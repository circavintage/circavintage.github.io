ABOUT CIRCAVINTAGE

Circa Vintage was first developed as an online shop in 2006 by Anne Morales selling vintage collections of her grandparents. From the usual clothes to collectibles such as vinyl, old recorders and posters, the shop stood still as an online shop catering services to buy and sell vintage materials all over Manila and some regions.
